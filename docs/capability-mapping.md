## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:open-book" width="28" height="28" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.3em;"></iconify-icon> Reading the Matrix

The matrix below condenses the full [Appendix]({{ site.baseurl }}/docs/appendix) technology mapping into a scannable dot-matrix view organized in three visual zones:

| Zone | Columns | What It Shows |
|:-----|:--------|:--------------|
| **Product** | 1 | Flip-card with the product or service name (click to reveal a short description of what it controls, monitors, or integrates with) |
| **Typical Owners** | 2 – 13 | A <span style="color:#00d4ff">cyan dot</span> appears in each organizational-owner column that typically operates or governs the capability |
| **Capability Themes** | 14 – 22 | A <span style="color:#f0883e">gold dot</span> appears for each control-capability theme the product maps to |

{: .note }
> **Tip:** Hover over any column header to see the full label. Click a product card to flip it and read the detailed description; click again to flip back.
